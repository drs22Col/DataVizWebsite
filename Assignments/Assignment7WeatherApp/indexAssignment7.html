The Music and Motion Weather App

This app is designed to take advantage of the sound and animation possibilities of p5 libraries. Depending on if it is sunny or cloudy, snowing or raining, you will hear a different song and see a different animation. And the song for the rain animation will also change if there's a thunderstorm.

Possible cities to try for different effects: Aomori (gets the most snow in the world), Yuma (in Arizona, and very sunny), Mawsynram (typically rainy), Buffalo (cloudy), and Kampala (a city in Africa where thunder is heard on average 242 days of the year). If there is no thunderstorm in Kampala today, you can go to http://en.blitzortung.org/live_lightning_maps.php for a live lightning map, and then choose cities in the states or countries where lightning is striking.

To pause a song, simply click your mouse. Or if it's a favorite, turn up the volume!
